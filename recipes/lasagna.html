Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it! Making lasagna can be time-consuming,

but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar, spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Allow the lasagna to reset before serving.
